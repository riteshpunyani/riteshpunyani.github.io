Prompt Engineering

The Art of Talking to AI

AI models are powerful, but their effectiveness depends heavily on how you talk to them. A precise prompt delivers accurate, tailored, professional results.

Essential 01
Clarity & Context
Be specific. Provide background and assumptions. E.g., "Assume Indian corporate regulations and professional communication standards."
Essential 02
Role Assignment
Tell the AI who it should be. E.g., "You are a fraud investigator. Draft a case summary…" — this shapes the entire output quality.
Essential 03
Chain-of-Thought
Ask AI to explain its reasoning step by step. Especially useful for audits, logic-heavy tasks, and investigation scenarios.
Essential 04
Few-Shot Prompting
Provide 2–3 examples of Q&A before your real question — like a mini-training set — to get precisely calibrated outputs.

🧑‍💻
Hack 1: Prompt Engineer Mode
Make AI act as your prompt consultant. It asks clarifying questions and rewrites your rough query into a sharper, more powerful prompt — reducing vagueness and avoiding hallucinations.
🔁
Hack 2: Two-Model Review Loop
Model A generates the response. Model B reviews it for accuracy and completeness. Adds a peer-review layer — great for compliance drafts, professional documents, and financial analysis.
🎛️
Master Prompt
A single, reusable prompt capturing your style, rules, and preferences — your personal AI operating system. Think: "You are my productivity co-pilot. Always respond with clarity and professionalism."
🕴️
Meeting Minutes (MoM) Prompt
Makes AI act as a corporate MoM drafter with zero assumptions. Guarantees meeting records that are complete, neutral, and audit-proof — ideal for compliance and investigation work.
