📱 Mobile Automation

Your Phone on Autopilot

Your phone can do much more than calls, chats, and scrolling — it can automate your daily routines. From silencing calls at night to controlling smart devices, mobile automation makes life smarter and hands-free.

Android & Samsung

Mobile Automation Tools

Task automation, smart home control, and device management — all from your phone.

Android
Tool 01
🤖
Tasker
The Swiss Army Knife of Android automation. Automates nearly everything based on triggers — time, location, Wi-Fi, battery, and more.
  • Turn on Do Not Disturb automatically at bedtime
  • Start Spotify when headphones are plugged in
  • Auto-send "I've reached" text when you arrive at work
🤖 AI Boost: "Create a Tasker profile that dims screen when battery < 20%." — GPT generates scripts instantly.
✅ Why it matters: Unmatched flexibility — automate virtually any phone action.
Android
Tool 02
📲
MacroDroid
A beginner-friendly alternative to Tasker with a simple "If this, then that" interface — no scripting knowledge needed.
  • Auto-disable mobile data when battery is low
  • Start fitness app when motion sensors detect a run
  • Mute phone automatically when connected to office Wi-Fi
🤖 AI Boost: GPT suggests ready-to-use MacroDroid recipes tailored to your daily routine.
✅ Why it matters: Automation for non-technical users — simple and visual.
Cross-Platform
Tool 03
🔄
IFTTT
A cloud-connected automation app that links mobile apps with hundreds of online services.
  • Auto-upload photos to Google Drive
  • Send yourself an email if you miss a call
  • Turn on lights when you arrive home
🤖 AI Boost: "Send me a WhatsApp message if tomorrow's forecast has rain." — New applet ideas generated by GPT.
✅ Why it matters: Connects mobile life with cloud services seamlessly.
Android
Tool 04
📊
Automate (LlamaLab)
A flowchart-style automation app that lets you build automations visually instead of coding.
  • Auto-launch Google Maps when Bluetooth connects to your car
  • Toggle Wi-Fi on/off based on location
  • Schedule reminders with conditions (only if calendar is free)
🤖 AI Boost: GPT explains complex flow blocks and generates custom visual flow designs.
✅ Why it matters: Visual, no-code automation — great for beginners.
Samsung
Tool 05
🔁
Samsung Modes & Routines
Built-in Samsung feature for context-based phone automation — switch modes automatically based on time, location, or activity.
  • Bedtime mode: mute notifications + dim screen automatically
  • Work mode: Wi-Fi on, ringer silent, productivity apps open
  • Fitness mode: auto-starts when workout is detected
🤖 AI Boost: GPT helps design multi-condition routines for your daily schedule.
✅ Why it matters: Zero-effort automation built right into Samsung devices.
Samsung IoT
Tool 06
🏠
SmartThings & Smart Life
Samsung's IoT platform for managing smart homes — lights, fans, AC, locks, and security cameras, all from one app.
  • Automate lights and AC based on schedule
  • Lock doors automatically at night
  • Get alerts from security cameras on your phone
🤖 AI Boost: "Turn off all lights, lock doors, and start AC at 10 PM." — GPT designs the full routine.
✅ Why it matters: Turns your home into a smart, automated environment.

⚡ Phone on Autopilot

From waking you up, managing your commute, to locking your doors — mobile automation keeps life running smoothly on autopilot.
