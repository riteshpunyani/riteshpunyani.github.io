☁️ Cloud & Integration

Connect Every App,
Automate Everything

Not all automation lives on your PC or phone — the most powerful tools run in the cloud. Think of them as automation highways: data flows from one app → transformed → lands in another, hands-free.

5 Platforms

Cloud & Integration Platforms

From no-code connectors to open-source powerhouses — cloud automation at every level.

Platform 01
⚡
Zapier
One of the most popular cloud automation platforms — connects 5,000+ apps without writing a single line of code.
  • Save Gmail attachments to Google Drive automatically
  • Add Typeform responses to Google Sheets instantly
  • Send Slack notifications when a new deal closes in HubSpot
🤖 AI Boost: "Create a Zap that sends me a daily summary of my calendar + unread emails." — GPT drafts the workflow.
✅ Why it matters: The easiest entry point to cloud automation — no technical knowledge needed.
Platform 02
🔄
Make.com (Integromat)
A visual automation builder for advanced users — more powerful than Zapier for complex, multi-step automations with conditional logic.
  • Sync new Shopify orders with an inventory system
  • Extract info from emails and update Airtable records
  • Post social media updates across multiple platforms simultaneously
🤖 AI Boost: Summarise customer reviews with GPT → push results into Power BI dashboards automatically.
✅ Why it matters: More powerful than Zapier for complex, branching workflows.
Platform 03
⚙️
n8n (Open Source)
An open-source automation powerhouse — self-hosted or cloud, with no vendor lock-in and deep AI integration capabilities.
  • Build your own "Zapier-like" workflows with full control
  • Connect databases, APIs, and cloud apps in one flow
  • Integrate AI models (OpenAI, Hugging Face) into workflows
🤖 AI Boost: "New lead in CRM → analyse sentiment with AI → notify sales on Slack." — GPT writes the entire flow.
✅ Why it matters: Developer-friendly, unlimited, and free to self-host forever.
Platform 04
☁️
MultCloud
A cloud storage manager that links multiple drives — sync and manage files across Google Drive, OneDrive, Dropbox, and more from one dashboard.
  • Sync Google Drive with OneDrive automatically
  • Auto-backup Dropbox files to MEGA
  • Manage all cloud accounts from a single interface
🤖 AI Boost: "Move any file older than 30 days to archive folder." — GPT writes the rule, MultCloud executes it.
✅ Why it matters: A lifesaver for professionals juggling multiple cloud accounts.
Platform 05
📂
FolderSync (Android)
Automates syncing files between phone storage and cloud services — keeping files available offline and across all your devices.
  • Auto-backup phone photos to Google Drive or Dropbox
  • Sync work documents between mobile and OneDrive
  • Keep offline copies of important cloud folders
🤖 AI Boost: "Keep last 30 days' files offline, delete older to save space." — GPT designs the sync strategy.
✅ Why it matters: Ensures files are always available across all your devices.
