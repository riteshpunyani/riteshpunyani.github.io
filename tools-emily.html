How It Works

Emily's 4-Step Intelligence

Step 01
🗣️
Voice Understanding
Emily interprets natural language — "Can I get a cleaning appointment this Friday?" She extracts intent, date, and service type instantly using NLP.
Step 02
💬
Context-Aware Responses
Unlike scripted bots, Emily responds based on context and tone — if a customer sounds worried, she acknowledges concern before helping.
Step 03
📆
Real-Time Booking
Emily checks the live Google Calendar, lists available slots, confirms availability, books directly, and logs customer details in Google Sheets.
Step 04
⚙️
No-Code Integration
The entire system is built using no-code and low-code tools — rapid iteration, easy expansion, zero fragile dependencies.
