🌐 Browser & Web Automation

Automate Your
Browser & Web Tasks

The internet is full of repetitive tasks — logging in, filling forms, downloading files, checking updates. Browser automation lets you delegate these to scripts, extensions, and bots so you can focus on smarter work.

5 Tools Covered

Browser & Web Automation Tools

From Chrome extensions to AI-powered agentic browsers — the full spectrum of web automation.

Tool 01
🧩
Chrome Extensions
Mini-tools that extend Chrome's functionality for automation, productivity, and customisation — installed in seconds, zero technical knowledge needed.
  • Auto-fill repetitive forms (job applications, expense claims, surveys)
  • Save all open tabs as a session and reopen later
  • Extract all links or emails from a webpage automatically
  • Block distractions or schedule website access
🤖 AI Boost: Ask GPT to suggest the best extensions for your workflow. Use Merlin or Monica to bring AI directly into your browser.
✅ Why it matters: Turns your browser into a productivity hub tailored to your needs.
Tool 02
🧑‍💻
Tampermonkey
A powerful user-script manager that lets you inject custom JavaScript into any website — giving you superpowers on any page.
  • Skip YouTube ads automatically
  • Auto-reply scripts for chat or forum platforms
  • Reformat cluttered webpages for readability
  • Scrape structured data without expensive tools
🤖 AI Boost: "Write a Tampermonkey script to auto-click 'Next' on a survey page." — GPT generates it in seconds.
✅ Why it matters: Customise the web exactly how you want it — on any site.
Tool 03
🔗
Webhooks & Integrations
Webhooks are "internet triggers" — they send data from one app to another in real time, silently connecting services without manual effort.
  • Send alerts when a Google Form is submitted
  • Track e-commerce orders in a dashboard automatically
  • Update spreadsheets when new data is posted online
  • Get instant Slack/Teams notifications from web apps
🤖 AI Boost: "Write a webhook that sends Gmail attachments to Google Drive automatically." — API snippets generated instantly.
✅ Why it matters: The backbone of app-to-app automation — silently connecting everything.
Tool 04
🕵️
Temp Mail & WebAlert XP
Specialised utilities for disposable emails and webpage monitoring — lightweight but extremely useful for testing and tracking.
  • Temp Mail: Create disposable emails for testing or product trials
  • Temp Mail: Avoid spam in your real inbox
  • WebAlert XP: Monitor webpages for stock status, exam results, or price changes
  • Get notified instantly when a page updates
🤖 AI Boost: "Notify me if flight prices drop below ₹5,000" — AI analyses changes and triggers only relevant alerts.
✅ Why it matters: Lightweight tools that punch well above their weight for everyday monitoring.
Tool 05
🌐
Agentic Browsers — The Future
Browsers (like Comet, Atlas, or BrowserGPT) that integrate AI agents directly — they don't just follow instructions, they understand intent, take actions, and reason through tasks in real time.
  • Auto-navigate websites to collect data or fill forms
  • Log into portals, upload files, and verify submissions
  • Run multi-step workflows: "Check new RBI policies and summarise updates"
🤖 AI Boost: Combine with GPT + n8n to build autonomous compliance agents that browse, extract, and summarise automatically.
✅ Why it matters: Merges web navigation with AI reasoning — the next generation of automation.

⚡ Browser Automation = Work Smarter, Not Harder

The browser is where most office work happens. With extensions, scripts, and webhooks, you can save hours of repetitive clicking and connect the web to your personal workflow.
