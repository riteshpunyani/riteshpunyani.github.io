About Me

Integrity with Innovation

I am a Chartered Accountant with over 18 years of experience in fraud investigations, ethics, and compliance. Currently serving as COBC Lead at Reliance Consumer Products Limited (RCPL), where I am setting up the Code of Business Conduct function from scratch and driving ethical culture across the organisation.

Previously Senior Manager, Ethics & Compliance at PwC, and before that spent 12 years at The Coca-Cola Company leading Fraud & Compliance across South-West Asia — India, Nepal, Bangladesh, and Sri Lanka. I drove the Fraud Management Framework across the INSWA region and also led internal controls, SOX compliance, and policy governance for the Microsoft Dynamics 365 ERP rollout in international markets.

Early career at Deloitte and Aviva Life Insurance in fraud investigations, compliance reviews, and white-collar crime assignments.

Deeply passionate about AI, automation, and data analytics as force multipliers — building practical tools that simplify complexity and improve efficiency exponentially.

"From leading Coca-Cola's regional fraud and compliance vertical across four countries — to setting up the COBC function at RCPL — my mission remains constant: combining ethics, compliance, and technology to build trust in business."
