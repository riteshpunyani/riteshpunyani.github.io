🤖 Custom GPTs

Purpose-Built AI Assistants

Specialised AI assistants configured with defined roles, structured instructions, and guardrails — delivering accurate, consistent, and context-aware outputs for specific professional use cases.

Concept

What Are Custom GPTs?

Unlike generic AI, Custom GPTs are trained for a specific role — acting like AI employees with defined expertise.

🧠
Defined Role & Persona
e.g., Fraud Investigator, Compliance Advisor, Prompt Engineer
📋
Structured Instructions
Rules on how to respond, what to include/exclude, and edge case handling
🎯
Use-Case Focus
Designed for one specific task — not everything to everyone
🔒
Constraints & Guardrails
Ensures accuracy, reliability, and ethical usage — critical in compliance contexts
