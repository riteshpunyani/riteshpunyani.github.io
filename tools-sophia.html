"Sophia" —
AI Order-Taking Assistant

An AI-powered voice assistant that receives customer orders naturally, validates real-time inventory, and logs structured orders automatically — zero manual entry, zero errors, 24×7.

The Problem

Why Sophia Was Built

📞
Missed Calls
Missed calls = missed revenue. Retail businesses lose orders every day.
❌
Manual Errors
Misheard items, wrong quantities, forgotten orders — costly and avoidable.
📦
No Inventory Check
False promises due to no real-time availability validation before confirming.
⏳
Delayed Confirmations
Customers wait for callbacks. Poor experience, lost trust.
How It Works

Sophia's 5-Step Process

Step 01
🗣️
Natural Language Understanding
"I want 5 apples and 2 mangoes for tomorrow evening." Sophia extracts products, quantities, and delivery time — no rigid commands needed.
Step 02
📦
Real-Time Inventory Check
Before confirming, Sophia checks live inventory in Google Sheets — identifying unavailable items and informing the customer instantly.
Step 03
💬
Intelligent Response
If all items available → confirms confidently. If something is unavailable → clearly informs. Always polite, never robotic.
Step 04
📝
Structured Order Logging
Automatically records customer name, phone, outlet, items, quantities, timestamp, and delivery time into Google Sheets — zero manual typing.
Step 05
⚙️
No-Code Architecture
Built entirely with no-code and low-code tools — scalable, auditable, and easily extendable to WhatsApp, SMS, or website chat.
